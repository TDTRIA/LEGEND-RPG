Devlog 008 / Stability Refactor and Road Systems

Stability Refactor and Road Systems

v0.6.4 was the safety pass after the public build push. The main game file was restored after an accidental stub overwrite, shared version labels reported correctly, Save Vault caught up, and new refactor files were checked before being activated.

The goal was stability first: keep LEGEND playable while preparing modular systems for tutorial, Sella, NPC metadata, Old Road events, and future travel expansion.
