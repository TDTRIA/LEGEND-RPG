The 10-Minute Route

This is the main route we want people to test first.

1

Create a character

Pick any origin, class, and keepsake. Notice whether the choices feel interesting or confusing.

2

Enter Ashmere

Check whether the first town menu feels manageable instead of overwhelming.

3

Open Settings

Try larger text, bolder text, high contrast, simplified ASCII, or reduced motion if anything feels hard to read.

4

Talk to Mara

Go to People of Ashmere and speak with Mara Vell. Then return to town and see what unlocked.

5

Explore Old Road

Start The First Road and complete one event or battle. Return to Ashmere afterward.

6

Check the town again

Look for new options such as the Inn, Trading Post, Ledger Hall, Archive Hall, or Save Vault.

What We Actually Need To Know

ConfusionReadabilityPacingUnlocksBugs
  • Did you know what to do first?
  • Did the locked menu options make sense?
  • Did the town feel less overwhelming than a giant menu?
  • Did accessibility settings help?
  • Did anything fail to click, save, load, or respond?
  • Did The First Road make you want to keep playing?

One clear complaint is more useful than ten vague compliments. “I got confused after talking to Mara” is great feedback.

Known v0.6 Tradeoff

v0.6 is a refactor build. Some v0.5.1 depth may feel leaner while the project moves into modular files. That is expected, but still worth reporting if something feels missing.
