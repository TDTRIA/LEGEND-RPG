Devlog 005 / Recovered Build v0.5

The First Road

v0.5 is a depth patch for the first 15 minutes of Legend. Instead of only adding more surface content, this update makes the first route, early combat, skills, and town preparation feel more meaningful.

      ___|___ .   . ___|___
  ____/   |   \_____/   |   \____
      THE FIRST ROAD

v0.5 is about making the opening loop stronger: leave Ashmere, survive the Old Road, collect Road Tokens, prepare at the inn, and return with a reason to keep going.

Major additions

  • Renamed the main beginner loop to The First Road.
  • Added Road Tokens as an early route objective.
  • Added a new objective to bring 3 Road Tokens to Old Brenn.
  • Added Ashmere Inn for resting, buying camp supplies, and buying road meals.
  • Added Camp Supplies as an economy sink and exploration survival item.
  • Changed weapons to steadier damage ranges instead of extreme random rolls.
  • Added enemy traits such as Sneaky, Charge, Drain, Stonehide, Unstable, Heavy, and Skittish.
  • Added mastery perks so skill mastery starts to matter.
  • Improved quest tracking with clearer current-objective text.

Why this update matters

The goal is to make the first play session feel stronger. Players should now have clearer goals, better reasons to prepare before exploring, and more readable combat outcomes.

What comes next

The next update should build on this by adding more route-specific events, more NPC reactions after quests, deeper crafting loops, and stronger Hollow Keep progression.
